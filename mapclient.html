class mapclient.PreferencesWindow[source]¶

The preferences window is the owner of everything else in the program. It should not be instantiated more than once.

Class attributes:
 
  • update_signal (PySide.QtCore.Signal) – When emitted (see PySide.QtCore.Signal.emit()),

    tells the LocationWorker to get the user’s new location (LocationWorker.get_location())

  • offline_signal (PySide.QtCore.Signal) – When emitted (see PySide.QtCore.Signal.emit()),

    tells the LocationWorker to stop sending stuff to the server (LocationWorker.stop_working())

  • post_signal (PySide.QtCore.Signal(api.Location)) – When emitted

    (see PySide.QtCore.Signal.emit()) with a api.Location instance, tells the LocationWorker to bind the current signal strength to the passed-in location and to update the user’s location on the map (LocationWorker.post_location())
